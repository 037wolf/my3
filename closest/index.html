Date of issue

The Nissan GT-R R35 was introduced in 2007 and became the fifth generation of the legendary Nissan Skyline GT-R.

Characteristics

The GT-R R35 is powered by a 3.8-liter turbocharged V6 engine, which generates an impressive output of around 565 horsepower in the Nismo version.

Speed

The ATTESA E-TS all-wheel-drive system and intelligent engine management have made the GT-R R35 one of the fastest production cars on the track, capable of accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in less than 3 seconds.
